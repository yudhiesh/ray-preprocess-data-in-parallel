'Eat Your F**king Food' Is The Parenting Anthem You Didn't Know You Needed

Too real.
By 
Caroline Bologna

Senior Reporter, HuffPost Life

08/11/2017 10:19am EDT

The funny ladies of The Pump And Dump Show are known for their original songs about the stresses of parenting.

“Eat Your F**king Food” goes right along with that theme. The song is a mother’s lament with a pretty self-explanatory title. While the verses refer to the struggle of feeding pureed food to a baby specifically, the chorus can apply to a whole delightful range of ages.

And it’s catchy to boot.
